Common Questions

Is solar expensive to install?

While the upfront cost can seem high, our financing options allow you to switch to solar for $0 down. The monthly savings on your bill often cover the EMI payments.

Does it work on cloudy days?

Yes, solar panels still generate energy from diffuse sunlight on cloudy days, though at a lower efficiency. Our systems are optimized for maximum yield even in less-than-perfect weather.

How much maintenance is required?

Solar systems have no moving parts and require very little maintenance. We recommend a basic cleaning once or twice a year, which we can handle for you.

Is there a warranty?

Absolutely. We provide a 25-year performance warranty and a 10-year hardware warranty on all our installations.
